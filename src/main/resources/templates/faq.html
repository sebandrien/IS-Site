FAQ

1. Who are we?

Illustrious Steel is a steel refinement company dedicated to delivering cutting-edge steel manufacturing solutions to our diverse international partners worldwide. Our commitment to quality and innovation sets us apart, ensuring the highest standards in every aspect of our operations.

2. How does this site work?

As a valued client-user, you gain access to our maintained database of current steel & rail inventory. By clicking on the button provided on the landing page, you'll be directed to a page where you can view our steel and rail inventories. Explore our inventory with ease and streamline your interactions with Illustrious Steel.

3. What powers this site?

This site is powered by a dynamic blend of technologies. On the frontend, we leverage HTML, CSS, and Javascript to craft a seamless and engaging user experience. Behind the scenes, Java serves as the robust backbone, ensuring the functionality and responsiveness of our platform. Additionally, MySQL plays a crucial role as the database management system, efficiently handling and organizing the wealth of information that fuels our operations, with MySQL Workbench being used as a database management tool.
